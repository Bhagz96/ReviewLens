How it works

Three steps to insights

From Google Maps to competitive intelligence in under a minute.

1

Create a SerpAPI account

Sign up at serpapi.com — the free tier includes 100 searches. Copy your API key from the dashboard and paste it above.

2

Find businesses on Google Maps

Search for any business on Google Maps, click its listing to open the place page, then copy the full URL from your address bar.

3

Paste URLs and run

Add up to 5 Google Maps URLs. The first is your primary business — the others are competitors. Hit Run to get your dashboard.
